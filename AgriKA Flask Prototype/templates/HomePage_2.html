About Us

AgriKA is an innovative platform dedicated to transforming precision agriculture. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and agriculture, ensuring a smarter and more efficient future for the farming industry.

Yield Prediction

Our yield prediction system leverages a CNN-LSTM model to estimate rice yields by utilizing satellite images and weather data as key features. With real-time data updates every five days, our platform ensures that farmers and agricultural stakeholders have the most up-to-date information for decision-making.
